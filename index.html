Our fall retreat will be held on the weekend of Friday, September 22 through Sunday, September 24 at Cormaria Retreat House in Sag Harbor, NY. We will be making some changes in the format of the retreat so as to get more involvement from the attendees.

The retreat will start at 8PM on Friday and will end before noon on Sunday. The cost is $275 and a deposit of $100 payable with check is required. The balance of $175 will be collected on Friday evening. The deposit is non-refundable unless you cancel at least five days prior to the start of the retreat weekend. If you would like to pay via an electronic money transfer, please contact Kevin (kbutscher@worldwise.com).

All reservations are on a first come first served basis. Confirmation emails will be sent. If your deposit is not received in time your check will be returned to you.

A response to this email is not required if you are not attending this retreat unless, of course, you are inclined to donate to our scholarship fund which is used to help those who cannot afford the retreat fee.

You can register and pay your deposit and/or scholarship donation only by check, money order, or through Kevin (kbutscher@worldwise.com).
